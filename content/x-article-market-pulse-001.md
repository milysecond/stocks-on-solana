## The setup

Tokenized equities let you trade exposure to real-world stocks and ETFs on Solana. Same ticker idea as the cash market, different venue, different liquidity, and often a premium or discount vs the NYSE/NASDAQ print.

That gap is the whole game. On-chain price is not always the real stock price. Liquidity is not always deep. Volume can concentrate in a handful of names while hundreds of tickers sit thin.

[Stocks on Solana](https://stocksonsolana.com) is built for that tape: live prices, 24h change, volume, liquidity, and a discount/mark column vs the real-world quote.

## What the discount column is for

On-chain tokens can trade rich or cheap to the cash equity.

- Green (discount): on-chain cheaper than the real stock
- Red (premium): on-chain richer than the real stock

Use it before size. Thin books print fake premiums. High volume and a tight mark is cleaner than a big percent move on almost no volume.
